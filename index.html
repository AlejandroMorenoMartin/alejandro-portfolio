Skills

These are the areas that empower me to engage in design.

Iterating

Craft compelling designs, iterating with precision to bring ideas to life and enhance user experiences.

Prototyping

Transform concepts into interactive prototypes, fostering a tangible feel for seamless user interactions.

Visual design

Infuse aesthetic appeal into interfaces, blending creativity and usability to captivate users attention.

Interactions

Meticulously refine micro-interactions, ensuring every detail contributes to a delightful user journey.

Design systems

Architect cohesive design systems, promoting consistency and scalability for a harmonized user experience.

Data structuring

Structure content strategically, creating intuitive information pathways that enhance user navigation.

Communication

I try to convey as accurately as possible my decision-making process and my progress on a day-to-day basis.

Problem solving

I like to have an overview to be able to focus on specifics and propose effective solutions.

Research

I focus my work based on the objectivity obtained from thorough research and compelling evidence.

Testing

Verify that the proposed designs are accepted, understood, and enjoyed by users.

Toolstack

As a designer, I strive to stay up-to-date on new technologies and tools.
