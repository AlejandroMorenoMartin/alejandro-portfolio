Skills

These are the areas that empower me to engage in design.

User research

Understanding users' needs and preferences through the collection and analysis of data on their behavior.

Ideation

Achieved through collaborative brainstorming sessions that foster concept generation and exploration of multiple approaches.

Facilitation

Guiding effective and efficient collaboration among team members and stakeholders to achieve project goals.

Prototyping

Creating simplified, functional representations of a product to test and validate design concepts.

Design systems

Creating and maintaining a comprehensive set of design standards, guidelines, and reusable components.

Communication

Effectively conveying design concepts, ideas, and research findings to various stakeholders.
