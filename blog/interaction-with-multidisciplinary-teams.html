Interaction with multi-disciplinary teams

Get to know the various roles that interact with design.

Alejandro Moreno

5 min read - Jan, 2023

Engineers

UX Researchers

Program Managers

Product Leads

Colleagues

Team

UX design is a highly collaborative field where designers typically work in multidisciplinary teams. These individuals often include engineers, researchers, project managers, leads, fellow designers, and others.

Multidisciplinary teams come in different shapes and sizes, depending on the organization you work for. But, in general, as a designer, you'll have to collaborate with:

Engineers

They are responsible for translating your designs into functional experiences. They are invaluable when evaluating designs in terms of technical feasibility. Working efficiently with them is an essential step for the successful completion of projects.

UX Researchers

They aim to understand what users expect from your product. They use methods such as observation, interviews, and surveys to grasp user perspectives. Observations in this regard will help make the best design decisions throughout the process.

In small companies or startups, you may be in charge of these research activities, while larger companies have specialized designers in this field.

Program Managers

They ensure clear and smooth communication among team members so that deadlines and objectives can be effectively met. They oversee, assist, and take charge of the completion of all tasks planned by team members. They should be seen as the main support source in the face of any unforeseen events or issues.

Product Leads

They are responsible for the product's success and communication with stakeholders. They define project goals and delivery deadlines so that other team members can focus solely on their tasks. They are also known as product managers.

Colleagues

Often, you won't be the only designer on the project. You'll have fellow designers working alongside you. Collaborating with other designers is a great way to learn.

Team

The goal of any company developing digital products is to have a team of individuals specialized in different areas who can collaborate and distribute work in a way that leads to the achievement of set objectives within determined deadlines.
