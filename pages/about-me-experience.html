Learn about my most recent work experiences.

Product designer at Geomites

Together with a team of designers and developers, I lead the UX and UI design in this project, through which we developed for IOS and Android platforms a video game for mobiles. I analyzed market leaders offering similar products, researched potential users and did the low-fi and high-fi design in Figma. Afterwards, I developed a prototype that allowed me to check with users through usability and A/B testing, how the design decisions were right or if they needed to be revised. With the prototype finished, I collaborated with the development team to bring the prototype into React. Finally, I developed a design system that allowed the product to be maintained and updated so that users felt comfortable using the prototype.

Product designer at Atipiko Tours

As part of a design and development team, collaborate with a fullstack engineer in the design and development of a website for a travel agency. Market research and analysis (users and competition). Creation of wireframes from low fi to high fi. Testing of the designs with users. Communication and close work with development for an effective implementation of the designs.

Feb 2023 - Oct 2023

Product designer at Senzo studio

Developed the website making sure that the final product was as expected by both stakeholders and users.
