About me

I love designing and creating digital experiences that solve real user problems and help companies achieve their goals. I consider myself a positive, proactive, methodical, self-taught person and I put my all into every project I undertake.

I collaborate closely with a team of five individuals, adhering to an agile methodology. My responsibilities encompass defining the design roadmap, aligning it with our technological stack, and addressing specific constraints. Primarily utilizing Figma and Adobe Illustrator, I am tasked with crafting the design system, prototypes, and wireframes to maintain consistency across diverse user flows. Additionally, I conduct comprehensive market research, heuristic analyses, and content evaluations to inform optimal decision-making
