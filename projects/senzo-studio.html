Introduction

In this project, I designed and developed a website for a VFX agency. Starting with in-depth user research and competitive analysis, I gained insights into the target audience and industry standards. I then created a comprehensive information architecture and cohesive design system, resulting in a website that highlights the agency's cutting-edge work while providing an intuitive and visually appealing user experience.

Research

I start the project by conducting research. The goal is to thoroughly understand the type of user, competing companies, and the market in which the company operates.

Survey

After analyzing the type of user for Senzo Studio, I prepared the following questions.

How do you anticipate using Senzo Studio's website in your professional workflow within the VFX industry?

Can you describe specific tasks or activities for which you would rely on Senzo Studio's website to enhance your work?

In your opinion, what are the key functionalities or features that a VFX studio's website should offer to cater to your needs effectively?

What types of content or resources would you consider essential for a VFX studio's website to provide in order to be a valuable tool for professionals in the field?

Considering your role and responsibilities in the VFX industry, what specific tools or functionalities do you believe should be easily accessible on the Senzo Studio website to streamline your tasks?

Insights

After asking the questions to the two users, I extracted the following information.
