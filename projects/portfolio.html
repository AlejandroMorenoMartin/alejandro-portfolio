Project - My portfolio

The objective of this project is to showcase the design and development process used to create the portfolio.

Jan 24

Product design

Front-end

4 weeks

Introduction

For this project, I'll use a framework commonly employed for design problem-solving. I'll use Figma for the design aspect and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for development, along with ChatGPT for support.

Framework Cheat Sheet

This framework uses 6 steps to solve the problem: why, who, when and where, what, solve and how.

Understand the goal (Why)

  • Why is this portfolio important?
  • What problem am I trying to solve?

"My professional profile is an opportunity to make myself known through the internet, positioning myself as a reference in the design field, and allowing easy collaboration and contact with interested parties."

Define the audience (Who)

  • What are the categories of people who have significantly different motivations for using this portfolio?

Understand the customer's context (When, where and feelings)

  • When. I assume that my target audience will use the portfolio when they are in a hiring or collaboration phase for a specific project or to fill a job position in a company that offers it.
  • Where. My target performs profile searches using their computers, mobiles, tablets from anywhere (office, home, gym, taking a walk or having a coffee). I expect both the search and the contact to take place in one of these situations.
  • Feelings. Anxious, stressed, expectant. Searching for profiles is a task that can be exhausting and stressful for both the recruiter and the hired.

Understand the customer's needs

  • Clarity and Organization. To access information swiftly and efficiently.
  • Demonstrated Skills. Seek concrete evidence of your product design expertise.
  • Relevant experience. Identify projects that highlight your ability to address specific challenges.
  • Culture and Adaptability. Find indicators of your personality and professional values.
  • Collaborative Opportunities. Evaluate potential for future collaboration on projects.
  • Learning and Development. Look for tutorials, case studies, or detailed project explanations that showcase your learning journey.
  • Impact and Results. Understand how your designs have contributed to the success of projects or products.
  • Strategy and Vision. Assess your capability to align design initiatives with broader business objectives.

(What) am I building?

  • What could I build to fulfill the customer's needs?

"Design and develop a professional portfolio that allows industry professionals to quickly and easily access my profile, facilitating immediate contact and collaboration."

Defining the product

The portfolio must exhibit exceptional organization and clarity, ensuring ease of reading and comprehension.

It should have a clear content structure to help alleviate any anxiety users may experience. It must include a project section showcasing my completed works and a testimonials section where colleagues and clients can share their experiences working with me. Additionally, it should offer a simple contact form, links to social media profiles, and updates on my professional engagements and academic certifications.

The portfolio should also feature a section highlighting readings that support my learning and a blog where I can share information to aid my professional development and assist others in the industry.

Sketching

Home - Welcome screen

The welcome section on the home page should be clean, clear and visually apparent. It should serve to make the user understand where they are, what they can do and reduce their anxiety as much as possible.

Home - Projects section

With the projects section, we allow the user to know part of our work, showing the ability as a designer, the design process with which I work and how I approach the problems to offer an optimal solution.

Home - Skills section

In this section the user can get an idea of the main skills I develop on a daily basis as a product designer.

Home - Testimonials section

To give more credibility to my work experiences and as a way to check the opinion of people who have worked with me before, with the testimonial section I help the user to solve this question.

How could I measure it?

Being able to evaluate the effectiveness of my portfolio will allow me to improve and adapt it in order to achieve the expected objectives. I will use the following KPI's to help me measure the effectiveness of my portfolio.

  • Website traffic. Number of monthly visits.
  • Session duration. Average time users spend on the portfolio.
  • Conversion rate. Percentage of users who contact after visiting the portfolio.
