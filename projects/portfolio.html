Introduction

For this project, I'll use a framework commonly employed for design problem-solving. I'll use Figma for the design aspect and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for development, along with ChatGPT for support.

Framework cheat sheet

This framework uses 6 steps to solve the problem: why, who, when and where, what, solve and how.

Understand the goal (Why)

My professional profile is an opportunity to make myself known through the internet, positioning myself as a reference in the design field, and allowing easy collaboration and contact with interested parties.

Define the audience (Who)

What are the categories of people who have significantly different motivations for using this portfolio?.

Unsterstand the customer's context (When, where and feelings)

When

Determine the feasibility of the tasks provided, including the number of clicks required for completion, execution time, and to gain a general understanding of how the user interacted with the product.

Where

I arranged a room for observing and recording the test, where the user, seated with the prototype in front, carried out the proposed tasks that were assigned.

Feelings

I selected 5 individuals, users from the tattoo industry with varying ages and levels of experience, for the observation and recording of the test.

(What) am I building?

Design and develop a professional portfolio that allows industry professionals to quickly and easily access my profile, facilitating immediate contact and collaboration.

Defining the product

The portfolio must exhibit exceptional organization and clarity, ensuring ease of reading and comprehension.

It should have a clear content structure to help alleviate any anxiety users may experience. It must include a project section showcasing my completed works and a testimonials section where colleagues and clients can share their experiences working with me. Additionally, it should offer a simple contact form, links to social media profiles, and updates on my professional engagements and academic certifications.

The portfolio should also feature a section highlighting readings that support my learning and a blog where I can share information to aid my professional development and assist others in the industry.
