Project - Tattoo paradise

A mobile application serves as a comprehensive guide, seamlessly connecting enthusiasts with diverse styles and artists in the dynamic world of tattoos.

June 23

Product design

Mobile design

6 weeks

Introduction

A groundbreaking mobile application designed to be the quintessential guide and connector in the vibrant world of tattoos, redefining the way individuals engage with and experience this unique art form.

Value proposition

Elevating the tattoo experience, this product offers centralized knowledge, immersive style exploration, direct artistic connections, trend insights, and community engagement, redefining how users navigate and interact within the dynamic tattoo culture.

Usability test

After analyzing the user and existing competitors in the market, I developed a prototype to conduct some tests and observe how real users interacted with it.

Goals

Determine the feasibility of the tasks provided, including the number of clicks required for completion, execution time, and to gain a general understanding of how the user interacted with the product.

Tasks

  • Access to study reviews 1
  • Activate all notifications
  • Access artist's designs 1
  • Send contact request to user 1
  • Rate the app
  • Access my conversations
  • Delete my account
  • Access tattoo styles
  • Rate the artist's first design 1
  • Access the care and hygiene guide
  • Log out

Method

I arranged a room for observing and recording the test, where the user, seated with the prototype in front, carried out the proposed tasks that were assigned.

Participants

I selected 5 individuals, users from the tattoo industry with varying ages and levels of experience.

Design decisions

After researching the market, understanding user needs, and establishing the design foundations of the project, I am now making some important design decisions that will consider all these variables, shaping the final product.

User and artist interaction

It is of vital importance that the product enables swift communication between users and artists. This will facilitate various types of interactions and open up different business opportunities.

Guide on tattoo styles

For less experienced users and those seeking to expand their knowledge, a style guide is provided. This guide empowers users to make informed decisions based on knowledge gained through the product.

Care and hygiene guide

To promote hygiene and post-tattoo care, users will have access to a guide that ensures a secure resolution of the post-tattoo stage.

Inspiration reel and variety of tattoos

Tattoo Paradise also features a reel system that allows users to view designs filtered by location and style. This way, they can find a source of inspiration.
